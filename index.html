Short bio

I am currently a Post-doctoral researcher at the Laboratoire Jean Kuntzmann of the University of Grenoble-Alpes. My main research interests are: applied stochastic calculus, singular diffusions, numerical approximations, hypocoercivity. I was formerly a PhD student at the Institut Elie Cartant de Lorraine under the supervision of Antoine Lejay and Denis Villemonais.
